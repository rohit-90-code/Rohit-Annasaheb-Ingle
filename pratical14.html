KAWASAKI NINJA H2R

A supercharged 998cc inline-four engine is Kawasaki's signature high-performance motor, notably powering the Ninja H2, H2R, and H2 SX, known for delivering extreme acceleration and power (over 200 HP) through a proprietary centrifugal supercharger, blending track performance with street usability in different models. Key Characteristics: Engine Type: 998cc, inline-four, liquid-cooled. Forced Induction: Features a mechanical centrifugal supercharger, supplying massive amounts of air for immense power. Performance: Produces over 200 horsepower (e.g., ~228 HP in the H2) and exceptional torque, enabling speeds over 300 km/h. Technology: Leverages Kawasaki Heavy Industries' gas turbine and gas engine tech to manage extreme performance and prevent knocking. Key Models: Ninja H2: Street-legal hypersport bike. Ninja H2R: Track-only version with even higher output (around 310 PS). Ninja H2 SX: A "hypersport" tourer focusing on balance, comfort, and fuel efficiency alongside power. Z H2: A "supernaked" bike sharing the same supercharged engine.
